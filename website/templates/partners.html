Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a large audience to apply?

Not at all. We welcome creators of all sizes. Quality of content matters more than follower count. Even micro-influencers with a dedicated photography audience are a great fit.

How do I track my referrals?

Once approved, you'll receive a unique referral code (e.g. ?ref=yourname). Any purchase made through that link is automatically attributed to you. We'll provide a dashboard with real-time stats.

When do I get paid?

Commissions are calculated monthly and paid out by the 15th of the following month. Minimum payout threshold is $20. We support PayPal and bank transfer.

Is there a limit on how much I can earn?

No caps, no limits. The more sales you refer, the more you earn. It's uncapped and ongoing for as long as your referral link is active.

Can I review honestly?

Absolutely. We want genuine, honest reviews. imagic is in active beta and we know it's not perfect yet — candid feedback helps us improve. We never ask for scripted or misleading content.

What if I'm not a photographer?

If your audience includes photographers or creative professionals, you're welcome to apply. We're open to tech reviewers, productivity creators, and anyone in the visual content space.
