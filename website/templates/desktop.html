Common questions
Everything you need to know before buying the imagic Desktop license.
Is this a one-time payment or a subscription?

100% one-time. You pay $10 once and own the license forever. No monthly charges, no annual renewal, no price increases. Unlike Adobe Lightroom at $9.99/month ($120/year), imagic Desktop is a single purchase.

How quickly do I receive my license key?

Your license key and installer download links are emailed automatically once Stripe confirms payment, typically within 1-2 minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it. The key is also displayed on the confirmation page.

Can I use the license on multiple computers?

Your license activates on one machine at a time. When you activate on a new device, the previous device is automatically deactivated. This makes switching to a new computer seamless, with no support ticket required, just activate on the new machine.

Which operating systems are supported?

imagic Desktop runs on Windows, macOS, and Linux. The same license key activates on any platform. You can also install via Python: pip install imagic.

What's the difference between Standard and the RawTherapee bundle?

The Standard installer includes imagic's full AI culling pipeline, editor, and JPEG/PNG export. The recommended RawTherapee bundle adds true RAW demosaicing via RawTherapee, which is the best option for photographers who want maximum quality from RAW files (CR2, NEF, ARW, etc.) with zero manual setup. Both are included at the same $10 price.

Do I need an Adobe account or internet connection?

No Adobe account, no Creative Cloud, no internet required after activation. imagic is completely independent of Adobe. All processing happens locally on your machine, so your photos stay private and speeds are limited only by your hardware.

What payment methods are accepted?

Payments are processed securely by Stripe. All major credit and debit cards are accepted: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and more. The checkout is PCI-compliant and your card details are never stored by imagic.

Is there a free trial before buying?

Yes. You can evaluate the desktop workflow before committing long term, and the license key unlocks unlimited photo processing. imagic is improved through active user feedback and direct iteration with the dev team, so the product keeps getting better based on real usage.
