Your personal AI editing assistant
imagic's AI doesn't replace you. It handles the tedious work so you can focus on the creative decisions that matter.
01

Intelligent scoring

Every photo is scored across 5 metrics: sharpness, exposure, noise, composition, and detail. The AI instantly separates keepers from rejects, so you start with the best instead of sifting through thousands.

02

Burst & duplicate detection

Shot 20 frames of the same scene? imagic finds visually identical photos and burst groups, then picks the sharpest automatically. You'll never manually compare near-identical shots again.

03

Smart crop & framing

Detects dead space, subjects, and rule-of-thirds alignment. Suggests optimal crop and framing with a confidence score. Accept the suggestion or ignore it, you're always in control.

04

Scene-aware profiles

Automatically detects whether a shot is a portrait, landscape, low-light scene, or high-contrast environment, and applies the perfect processing profile to match.

05

Feedback learning

This is what makes imagic truly unique: the AI tracks your keep/trash decisions, your edits, and your style preferences. Over time, it learns what you consider a great photo and weights its scoring accordingly. It's like training your own personal assistant.

06

You're always in control

Every AI suggestion can be overridden. Keep a photo the AI rejected. Trash one it liked. Adjust any edit. The AI adapts to your choices, it assists and never dictates.

Think of imagic as your editing intern. It does the grunt work, scoring, sorting, and flagging duplicates, and learns from every correction you make. After a few sessions, it knows your taste better than any preset ever could.

Frequently asked questions
Everything you need to know about imagic, the AI photo culling and editing software.
Is imagic really free?

imagic Desktop is available with a one-time $10 license key and no recurring fees. Instead of locking you into a subscription, the product keeps improving through direct feedback from photographers and a tight development loop focused on real workflow pain points.

Does imagic work on Mac, Windows, and Linux?

Yes. imagic runs natively on Windows, macOS, and Linux. You can download platform-specific installers or install it via pip: pip install imagic.

What RAW formats does imagic support?

imagic supports all major RAW formats: CR2 & CR3 (Canon), NEF (Nikon), ARW (Sony), RAF (Fujifilm), ORF (Olympus), RW2 (Panasonic), DNG (Universal/Adobe), PEF (Pentax), plus JPEG, PNG, TIFF, and WebP.

How does the AI photo culling work?

imagic's AI analyses every photo across 5 quality metrics: sharpness, exposure, noise, composition, and detail. Each photo gets an overall score. The AI automatically flags duplicates and burst groups, selecting the sharpest shot from each group. You can keep or trash photos with a single click, and the AI learns your preferences over time.

Can imagic replace Adobe Lightroom?

For most photographers, yes. imagic covers the full Lightroom workflow: import RAW files, AI-cull the best shots, batch edit with color grading and exposure adjustments, and export. The key advantage is imagic's built-in AI culling (Lightroom has no equivalent), plus it costs a one-time $10 instead of $9.99/month. For full RAW demosaicing, pair it with the optional RawTherapee integration.

How long does it take to cull 1,000 photos?

The AI analysis pass typically takes 30–90 seconds for 1,000 photos on a modern machine. Manually culling 1,000 photos in Lightroom takes 2–3 hours. imagic reduces culling time by up to 10×, meaning a typical wedding shoot of 3,000 photos takes minutes instead of a full day.

What's the difference between Desktop Standalone and Desktop + RawTherapee?

Desktop Standalone uses imagic's built-in processing engine for full AI culling, editing with color grades, and batch JPEG/PNG export. Desktop + RawTherapee adds true RAW demosaicing via RawTherapee for the highest possible output quality, ideal for serious RAW shooters. Both cost the same one-time license.

Do I need an internet connection to use imagic?

No. imagic processes everything 100% locally on your machine. No photos are ever uploaded to any cloud server. An internet connection is only needed for initial license key activation. This means your photos stay private and processing speeds are only limited by your hardware.

Does imagic learn my editing style?

Yes. imagic's feedback learning system tracks your keep/trash decisions, the manual edits you make, and the color grades you prefer. Over time, the AI adjusts its scoring weights to better reflect your personal taste, like training a photo editor who knows exactly what you look for. After a few sessions, the AI's suggestions align closely with your eye.

How does imagic improve over time?

imagic is built as a focused product, not as an editable codebase for end users. Improvements come from a strong community, direct bug reports, feature requests, and ongoing feedback to the dev team so the roadmap stays aligned with how photographers actually work.
