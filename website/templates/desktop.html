Installing imagic on macOS

Your download has started. Follow these steps to install and open the app.

1

Open the DMG

Once the download finishes, double-click imagic-desktop.dmg in your Downloads folder. You'll see the EULA — click Agree to continue.

2

Drag to Applications

In the window that opens, drag the imagic icon onto the Applications folder shortcut. This copies the app to your Mac.

3

First launch — right-click to open

Open Applications in Finder, find imagic, then right-click → Open. macOS may show a warning the first time because the app is from outside the App Store — click Open to confirm.

You only need to do this once. After the first launch, imagic opens normally with a double-click.
4

If blocked — allow in Privacy & Security

If macOS still won't open the app, go to System Settings → Privacy & Security, scroll down to the Security section and click Open Anyway next to the imagic message. Then try launching the app again.

This happens because imagic is distributed directly, not through the Mac App Store. It's safe to allow.
5

Activate with your product key

On first launch, enter the product key from your email to activate imagic. RawTherapee is already bundled inside — no extra setup needed.

Still seeing "app is damaged"? Open System Settings → Privacy & Security, scroll down and click Open Anyway next to the imagic message.
