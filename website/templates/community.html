How we build imagic

Every feature, fix, and priority comes from real photographer feedback. We don't build in isolation — we build with you. If something's broken, slow, or missing, we want to hear about it. The roadmap is public and driven by the people who actually use the tool.

imagic is indie software, not a VC-backed startup. We ship fast, listen closely, and care about the craft.
